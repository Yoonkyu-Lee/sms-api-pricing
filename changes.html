RULETwilio requires privacy policy and T&C URLs for new A2P 10DLC campaign registrations2026-06-30›
Starting June 30, 2026, Twilio's Messaging REST API rejects new A2P 10DLC campaign registrations that omit PrivacyPolicyUrl or TermsAndConditionsUrl fields.
